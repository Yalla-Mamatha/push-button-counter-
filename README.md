DESCRIPTION:A push button counter is a simple digital electronic circuit that counts the number of times a push button is pressed. It is widely used in educational projects, industrial equipment, electronic devices, and automation systems to keep track of events or operations. Every time the push button is pressed, the counter increases its count by one, and the current count is displayed using LEDs, a seven-segment display, or an LCD display.In conclusion, a push button counter is a useful and reliable digital circuit for counting events accurately. Its simple construction, low cost, and wide range of practical applications make it an essential circuit in electronics, industrial automation, and educational projects.
